social contract


rotting girls in a rotting world // the sound of 2024

"social contract" features songs that were largely written during what i would call one of the roughest parts of my life. i was struggling with feelings of derealization, was increasingly frustrated with the conditions of the world i live on, and was right off the heels of a friend group implosion.
being able to take the experiences i had during that period and make them into art has been so validating, both as a way to process said feelings, and to prove to myself that i can make proper, full-length songs.
