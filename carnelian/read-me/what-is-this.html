Welcome To My Computer! (or old iPhone, if you're on a phone)
A number of months ago I realized I've been firmly rooted in an "era" of sorts, with the repeated feelings I was feeling, and the songs I was making.
2024 was a really rough year for me in a lot of ways, and this whole aesthetic cycle has been a way for me to echo that feeling. This computer simulation is the final product of an idea I've had since the start, it started with wanting to do an ARG, but it's ending in a sort of non-fiction abstract vent display of myself. I've realized that I:

A) didn't have the oomph in me to write a whole story
B) thought that a brief picture into the real life of someone like me is story enough to be a good companion for the EP

This section of my website is meant to emulate a File Explorer, specifically one on *my* machine. It's meant to feel like snooping, being some place you shouldn't be. This is my machine, not yours... But I can't stop you from looking.

I also want to be clear that I'd like to use this spot for more than just weird self-indulgent art, but really I don't know what this is yet. It might be blogging, infodumping, art leaking, etc... The guidance I can offer for peeking at this time is:
